Frequently asked questions on Samadhan

1)Where can I register my Problem?

->You can open the Samadhan portal using the link www.samadhan.in and click on the "Login In/Register" tab to register your problems.

2)Can I register problem without Aadhaar card?

->No you need your aaddhar card number to register your issue.

3)Is online registration mandatory for registering my problem?

->Yes first you need to register yourself and then login with your registration details for registering your problem.

4)Which age groups can register their problems?

->Anyone who is above 18 years of age can register their problem.

5)Is there a mobile app that needs to be installed to register?

->No you just need to reigster your problems on website any your issue will be solved.

6)Is there any registration charge to be paid?

->No, there is no registration charge.

7)How can I find the nearest physical center?

->You can find nearest centeres on the Samadhan main page.

8)How much time it will take to solve the problem?

->The amount of time to solve any problem will be from minimum 1 day to maximum 7 days.

9)How will I know that my problem is solved?

->You will be emailed on your register email id that your query has been resolved.

10)How to register a complaint if my issue is not resolved?

->You can call on the helpline number given.
